What I'm Doing Now

A week ago I decided to take a hiatus from, well, everything.

I wanted to know how it would feel to have contact with nobody. No social media, messages, calls, news – nothing but my work and my own thoughts.

I'm one week in and already finding it deeply refreshing. I didn't realise how much I was being affected by all the noise until it wasn't there.

I feel a bit like a child again – waking up with no plans and just spending the day doing what makes me happy.

In other news, I've decided to pivot Yakk into an exciting new thing. I'll share more later.

For now I'm spending my days programming, reading, and enjoying solitude.
